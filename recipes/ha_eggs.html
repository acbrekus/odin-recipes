Heart Attack Eggs

Description

Ever felt the need for a good breakfast, and want an easy, fast recipe? Well, heart attack eggs are for you!

With just a pan, bacon, and eggs, you are on your way to a delicious start to your day!

Ingredients

  • Three Eggs
  • A pack of bacon
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Optional: An empty can

Steps

  1. Fill up your pan with bacon
  2. Set stove temperature to medium
  3. Cook bacon in pan, flip to evenly cook both sides
  4. Once bacon is browned, remove from pan and place bacon on paper towels to absorb grease
  5. Crack open some eggs and place in pan.
  6. Cook eggs sunny-style up, they should cook fast so pay close attention
  7. Remove eggs from pan, put on plate with bacon, and season
  8. You've done it! Enjoy your eggs and bacon
