Cereal Recipe for Dummies

Description

Literally a bowl of cereal. Why are you even here?

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A spoon
  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. First go to the store and purchase a cereal box of your choice and milk
  2. Next, go home and set a bowl on a table
  3. IMPORTANT: Place the cereal in the bowl first
  4. Now we add the milk.
    • If you prefer to eat like you're in the Sahara desert, don't add much if any milk
    • If you prefer to eat like you're sinking on the Titanic, pour milk to the brim of the bowl
    • If you prefer to eat like a sane person, put in an average amount of milk
  5. Now put the spoon in the bowl, pick up the cereal and put it in your mouth
  6. Chew the cereal with your teeth unless you enjoy choking
  7. You've done it! You've eaten a bowl of cereal. Your parents now owe me $20 for basic parenting lessons.
