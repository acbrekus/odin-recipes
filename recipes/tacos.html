Taco Recipe

^Not exactly these tacos though

Description

Who doesn't like tacos? If your hand is raised, kindly lower it. Tacos are delicious and can turn an awful day into a bittersweet one!

We're going to use ground beef for this recipe, but you can alternatively use chicken or shrimp if that's your fancy! Cooking with ground beef however is a good start on your taco journey.

Ingredients

  • A pound of ground beef
  • Taco shells
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • Seasoning mix

Steps

  1. Brown 1 pound of ground beef in a pan and drain
  2. Add 3/4 cup of water and seasoning
  3. Stir frequently for 3-5 minutes
  4. Fill taco shels with seasoned ground beef
  5. Optionally add other vegetables and toppings to your taco
  6. Enjoy your meal
